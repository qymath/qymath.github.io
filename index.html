About me

I am a Postdoctoral Assistant Professor in algebraic geometry at the University of Michigan beginning at August 2022.

Previously, I completed my Ph.d. at Stony Brook University in May 2022 under the guidance of Christian Schnell.

I am interested in Hodge theory, D-modules and I think about their applications in algebraic geometry.
